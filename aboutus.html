About Us
Welcome to Kimchi Korean Restaurant! We offer a wide selection of authentic Korean dishes, made with the freshest and highest quality ingredients. Check out our latest menu specials and featured items, and connect with us on Facebook to receive e-mails with our latest deals and coupons! Located in Oviedo, Florida, we offer an enjoyable dining experience, complete with free wi-fi and Korean television. If you're in a rush, call ahead and order our food to-go! Kimchi Korean Restaurant opened in November 2010 bringing authentic Korean food to the Orlando area. Kimchi offers traditional Korean cuisines including soups, stews, casseroles, seafood, Korean BBQ, noodles, and vegetarian dishes at reasonable prices. It is the perfect restaurant if you are looking to try something different!
